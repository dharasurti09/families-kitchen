Breakfast Recipes

Pancakes

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
Steps:
  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add egg and milk, stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan and pour batter.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip, and cook until golden.

Lunch Recipes

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients:
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • Butter
Steps:
  1. Butter the outside of both bread slices.
  2. Place cheese between the unbuttered sides.
  3. Grill in a pan until golden and melted.

Dinner Recipes

Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti
  • Minced meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion, garlic
  • Salt, pepper, herbs
Steps:
  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Cook onion and garlic in a pan.
  3. Add meat, cook until brown.
  4. Pour in tomato sauce and simmer.
  5. Serve sauce over spaghetti.
